Interpretation of results


GLIIT is divided into 4 categories for easier interpretation of the results. The first category are Strong inter-industrial trade tendencies, the second category are Weak inter-industrial trade tendencies, the third category are Weak intra-industrial trade tendencies, and the fourth category are Strong intra-industrial trade tendencies.


Interpretation of GLIIT results
Classification Explanation
0%<GLIIT<25% Strong inter-industrial trade tendencies
25%<GLIIT<50% Weak inter-industrial trade tendencies
50%<GLIIT<75% Weak intra-industrial trade tendencies
75%<GLIIT<100% Strong intra-industrial trade tendencies
